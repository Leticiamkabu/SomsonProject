Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions.
Have a question not listed? Feel free to contact us.

How do I schedule a cleaning service?
Scheduling a cleaning service is easy. You can either call our customer service number or book online through our website. Our team will then work with you to find a convenient time for the service.
What cleaning services do you offer?
We offer a wide range of cleaning services including residential cleaning, commercial cleaning, post-construction cleaning, carpet cleaning, emergency cleaning, and green cleaning.
How much does your cleaning service cost?
The cost of our cleaning service depends on various factors such as the size of the property, the type of cleaning required, and any additional services requested. Contact us for a personalized quote.
Are your cleaners insured and bonded?
Yes, all our cleaners are fully insured and bonded for your peace of mind. You can trust us to handle your property with care and professionalism.
Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?
No, we provide all the necessary cleaning supplies and equipment. Our professional cleaners come fully equipped to handle any cleaning job efficiently and effectively.
What happens if I need to reschedule?
If you need to reschedule your cleaning service, please contact us as soon as possible. We will do our best to accommodate your request and find a new appointment time that works for you.
